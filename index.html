About Us

Daallo Airline is committed to providing exceptional air travel experiences connecting key destinations with unmatched safety and comfort.

Established in 1991, Daallo Airline has grown to become one of the leading airlines in the region. Our fleet is modern and maintained to the highest international standards, ensuring safety and reliability on every flight. We offer convenient schedules, excellent customer service, and competitive fares tailored to meet the needs of both business and leisure travelers. Our mission is to connect communities and foster economic growth by providing dependable air transportation solutions.
